What We Do

First off, this isn't a formal business. Four Seasons Maps is an outlet for my mapping endeavours, a sort of portfolio for every random and/or contracted map I make. That's right, when you read "we" or "our", it's being used in its royal form. Hi, my name is Evan and I make maps *waves hand*. For years I've bounced around between different website ideas, unsure of how to consolidate projects like Appalachian Maps, Hike the Carolinas, 828Weather, and more. As fun as that's been, I've decided that Four Seasons Maps is where I will consolidate my work from here on out.

If you're interested, I do sell some maps on my Etsy shop. I'm pretty biased, but I like to think they look great printed on cardstock and hung in a frame. To view my selection, open the menu and navigate to the Etsy Shop page. For those of you with some QGIS experience, go check out the Mapping Tutorials page for some free demos of my most used map types.

I'm a fairly easy guy to reach on Twitter, less so on email and other social platforms. If you have mapping questions or an Etsy Shop map request, shoot me a DM @EFisherWX. Elsewhere I'm @EFisher828 on Instagram and evan.w.fisher@gmail.com on the good 'ole email.
